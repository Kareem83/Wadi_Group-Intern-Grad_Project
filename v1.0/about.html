Who We Are

The Heartbeat of Change & Prosperity

Wadi Group enjoys the heritage and expertise of more than half a century in the agribusiness industry throughout the MENA region. Launched in Egypt in 1984 with a small-scale poultry operation, today Wadi Group operates across 12 subsidiaries with ten brands over three distinct sectors.

We have turned the desert green, helped make poultry an affordable household staple, and successfully integrated industry across the supply chain — delivering what we promise as a testimony to our commitment to quality, sustainability, and food security.
