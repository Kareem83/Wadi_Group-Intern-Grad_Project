Sector 1 · Poultry

Dawagen

The semi-integrated poultry operation of Wadi Group. Dawagen specializes in breeding, hatching and selling broiler parents, commercial broiler and layer day-old chicks (DOCs) with superior genetic potential — backed by free, comprehensive after-sales veterinary support built over 50 years in the industry.

Katkoot El Wadi

The commercial brand for Wadi's local poultry operations, producing day-old chicks since 1986. Grown from 1.5M layer chicks in 1986 to over 10M today — more than 30% of local demand.

Visit site →

Inmaa Sudan

A joint venture with Al Nefeidi Group transferring Wadi's poultry expertise to Sudan — the country's sole grandparent operation, with 45% of the commercial broiler market.

Visit site →

Hi-Tec

Wadi Group's advanced veterinary laboratory, running diagnostics on disease and immunity levels, monitoring flock health and evaluating vaccination programs for Wadi and its customers.

Get in touch →
Sector 2 · Agriculture & Food

Mazareh

Wadi's agri-business division — land cultivation, food processing and fish farming, run to international best practice with a focus on organic technique and environmental sustainability. Rula and Wadi Food together form a fully integrated farm-to-shelf operation.

Wadi Food

Established 1986. Award-winning extra virgin olive oil, pickles, olives, tomato paste, vinegar and fava beans — "Proudly Grown in Egypt" and trusted across the Middle East.

Visit site →

Rula Farms

The agricultural backbone of Mazareh. Since 1986, grown from 7,000 olive trees on 30 hectares to over 2,000 hectares of organic olives, grapes, fruit, vegetables and marine fish.

Visit site →
Sector 3 · Industrial & Logistics

Sina'at

Wadi's industrial division, built to support the core poultry and agribusiness operations: feed manufacturing, feed additives, evaporative cooling, and grain-handling logistics by river and road.

Tabreed

Evaporative cooling pads that cut poultry-house and greenhouse temperatures by up to 20°C, boosting flock capacity and crop yield across Egypt and Turkey.

Visit site →

A'laf Al Wadi

Three feed mills producing poultry and large-animal feed — a 15% share of Egypt's feed market, with tailored formulas for every growth stage.

Visit site →

Haditha

Solvent-extracted soybean oil and high-protein soybean meal — a key protein source for poultry and ruminant feed.
