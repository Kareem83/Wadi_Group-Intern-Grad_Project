About Wadi Group

Half a century of building the MENA region's food chain — from a single poultry operation in 1984 to twelve subsidiaries across three integrated sectors.

Company Overview

Heritage, expertise and an appetite to lead.

Wadi Group enjoys the heritage and expertise of more than half a century in the agribusiness industry throughout the MENA region. Launched in Egypt in 1984 with a small-scale poultry operation, today Wadi Group operates in 12 subsidiaries with ten brands over three distinct sectors: Dawagen, Sina'at and Mazareh.

Every business unit feeds the next — breeding and feed support the farms, the farms supply the food factories, and cooling, storage and transport carry it all to market. It is one connected value chain, engineered to move quality from the soil to the shelf.

From Egyptian households and farmers to export partners across the Middle East, Wadi Group pairs deep agricultural know-how with modern industry to deliver trusted products at scale.
